MUGA

Make URLs Great Again.
Here's exactly what this extension does — and what it never will.
MUGA never acts behind your back. Nothing happens without your action.

1
Always on — no configuration needed
🧹
Strips 435 tracking parameters automatically UTMs, fbclid, gclid, msclkid, mc_cid, si (YouTube), eBay tracking tags, and more — removed before the page loads.
⚡
Redirects Google AMP pages to the real article AMP URLs are silently redirected to the canonical article so you always land on the actual site.
🚫
Blocks <a ping> tracking beacons Prevents the hidden ping requests advertisers use to log every link you click.
🔗
Unwraps redirect-wrapper URLs Extracts the real destination from tracking wrappers so you go straight to the target page.
📋
Right-click → Copy clean link Copies the cleaned URL to your clipboard without navigating anywhere.
2
How MUGA stays free — your choice

When you visit a supported store (Amazon, Booking.com, AliExpress…) and the link has no affiliate tag at all, MUGA can silently add ours. The price you pay is always identical — affiliate tags don't affect what you pay, they tell the store who referred you. We earn a small commission.

This is how we keep MUGA free and actively maintained.

What MUGA never does: replace or overwrite a tag that is already in a link. If a creator's or another affiliate's tag is there, MUGA leaves it alone. This is the exact opposite of what Honey did. MUGA only acts on links that have no tag at all. The source code is public — you can verify this yourself.

You can change all of this later in Settings at any time.
