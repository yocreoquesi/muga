MUGA — Make URLs Great Again · Version 1.0 · Last updated: 2026-03-22

MUGA is free and open-source software licensed under the GNU General Public License v3. All processing happens locally in your browser — no data is ever sent to any server.

1. What MUGA does

MUGA is a browser extension that:

  • Strips tracking parameters (UTMs, click IDs, and similar) from URLs before pages load
  • Redirects Google AMP pages to their canonical article URLs
  • Blocks <a ping> tracking beacons on link clicks
  • Unwraps redirect-wrapper URLs to take you directly to the destination
  • Optionally adds our affiliate tag to links that carry no affiliate tag, if you opt in

2. Affiliate model

MUGA is maintained by a small team. To keep the project alive and free, we participate in affiliate programs with a small number of stores (Amazon, Booking.com, AliExpress, and others).

If you opt in during setup, MUGA will add our affiliate tag to links that have no existing affiliate tag. This has no effect on the price you pay — affiliate tags tell the store who referred you, nothing more.

  • MUGA never replaces an affiliate tag that is already present in a link
  • MUGA never replaces a creator's or another affiliate's tag with ours
  • Affiliate injection only fires on explicitly supported stores listed in the source code
  • You can disable affiliate injection at any time in Settings

3. No data collection

MUGA does not collect, transmit, or store any browsing data. All URL processing happens exclusively inside your browser. We have no servers, no analytics, and no telemetry of any kind. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
