2. What MUGA does

MUGA is a browser extension that:

  • Strips tracking parameters (UTMs, click IDs, and similar) from URLs before pages load
  • Redirects Google AMP pages to their canonical article URLs
  • Blocks <a ping> tracking beacons on link clicks
  • Unwraps redirect-wrapper URLs to take you directly to the destination
  • Optionally adds our affiliate tag to links that carry no affiliate tag, if you have opted in

You have full control over every feature. MUGA never collects, sells, or shares your browsing data. Any feature that contacts an external service requires your explicit action.

3. Affiliate model and disclosure

MUGA is maintained by an independent developer. To keep the project alive and free, MUGA participates in affiliate programs operated by third-party stores, including but not limited to Amazon Associates and eBay Partner Network.

If you opted in during onboarding, MUGA adds our affiliate tag to URLs that contain no existing affiliate tag. This does not change the price you pay. Affiliate tags tell the store who referred the visit, nothing more. The developer may earn a small commission from qualifying purchases.

  • MUGA never replaces an affiliate tag that is already present in a URL
  • MUGA never overwrites a content creator's or another affiliate's tag
  • Affiliate tag injection only applies to the supported stores explicitly listed in the source code
  • You can disable affiliate tag injection at any time in the Extension's Settings, no questions asked
  • As an Amazon Associate, the developer earns from qualifying purchases. This disclosure is required by the Amazon Associates Program Operating Agreement
